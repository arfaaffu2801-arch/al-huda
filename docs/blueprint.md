# **App Name**: Rahma Hub

## Core Features:

- Prayer Time Notifications: Accurate prayer time calculations and customizable notifications based on user's location.
- Quranic Verses Display: Display daily Quranic verses with translations and transliteration.
- Du'a Recommendations: AI-powered tool to suggest relevant Du'as (supplications) based on user's current situation.
- Qibla Direction: Use device sensors to provide accurate Qibla direction.
- Islamic Calendar: Display of the Islamic (Hijri) calendar with important Islamic events.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Blue (#3B5998) to evoke a sense of trust and serenity, referencing the traditional colors associated with Islamic art.
- Background color: Light Gray (#F0F2F5), a subtle and unobtrusive background to ensure readability and focus on content.
- Accent color: Gold (#FFD700) to highlight important elements and call to actions, reminiscent of Islamic art's gold accents.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif font, gives an elegant, intellectual and contemporary feel.
- Use elegant, calligraphic-inspired icons for navigation and features.
- Clean, card-based layout with a focus on readability and ease of navigation.
- Subtle transitions and animations for a smooth, intuitive user experience.
